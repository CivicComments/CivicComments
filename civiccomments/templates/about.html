CivicComments.org is a for profit website aiming to inform citizens about civic issues and to encourage them to post their comments on the site. Note that the site is a .org because .com is taken.

The site is ran solely by Tim Clemans, featured in a New York Times Magazine cover story about police bodycam transparency. Tim started CivicComments after seeing a land use proposal notice. It doesn't give a url one could use to publicly post their comment. Instead people have to email the city and the comments are posted online on a per comment to download basis. CivicComments downloads those comments automatically and presents them on a single page.
